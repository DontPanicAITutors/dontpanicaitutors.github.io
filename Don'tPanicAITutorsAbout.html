About the Founders

Founded in 2024 by a team of passionate students, Don't Panic AI Tutors is Lawrence's first AI education provider dedicated to preparing students for the digital future.

Lawrence

Lawrence is a freshman at Lawrence Virtual High School with a passion for STEM, particularly Artificial Intelligence. He enjoys staying up-to-date with the latest AI news and breakthroughs, constantly seeking to expand his knowledge in this exciting field. In 2024, Dao and his team won the eCybermission National STEM Competition with a project on AI, and specifically ChatGPT, in schools. He and his team's actions led to the creation of Lawrence Public Schools's first Generative AI Task Force. In 2025, Dao qualified for the Great Plains Regional Junior Science and Humanities Symposium, where his research on AI student surveillance tools in schools garnered him a first place finish. He will go on to compete at nationals later this year (2025).

Daniel

Daniel is a 7th grader with a passion for programming, AI, and playing with Rubik's Cubes. His favorite subjects are math and history. The brothers passion for AI led them to create "Don't Panic AI Tutors". Dao helped his team win the 2024 eCybermission National STEM Competition. Additionally, Dao and his brother have presented their research to the district board of education numerous times. The idea for "Don't Panic AI Tutors" also netted the Dao bros a first place finish at the 2025 Douglas County YEC. Our Mission

Don't Panic AI Tutors was founded through a mix of our team's passion and our observation that our community lacks a dedicated AI program for such an important technology.

Our mission is to provide students with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed in a world increasingly shaped by AI. We believe that AI literacy is a critical skill for the future, and we are committed to helping students develop the skills they need to thrive in the digital age.

To do this, we made sure that we offer:

High-quality, personalized AI education programs for all learners, all ages, and skill levels!

Local expertise, designed specifically for Lawrence students by students who understand our community!

Hands-on learning experiences that engage students and help them develop real-world AI skills!

Support for Lawrence Public Schools (USD 497) AI fluency initiatives!

Occasional talks from local graduate student and experts to expose to students high level content, in cool ways!

Group courses allow students with the same interests to mingle and bulid connections with each other!
